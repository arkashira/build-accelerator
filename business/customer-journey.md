# Customer Journey Map for Build-Accelerator

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Developers encounter slow compilation times during their JavaScript/TypeScript projects, leading to frustration and inefficiency.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about potential solutions; existing tools do not effectively address their needs.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration, confusion, and a sense of urgency to find a solution.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Create informative content (blogs, videos) showcasing the benefits of Build-Accelerator; leverage social media and developer forums to spread awareness.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement on awareness content (e.g., blog views, video views).

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Developers actively search for solutions to improve compilation times and productivity.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelming number of options; difficulty in comparing features and benefits of different tools.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful yet overwhelmed; cautious about making the wrong choice.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide clear, concise comparison charts; offer case studies or testimonials from early adopters highlighting productivity gains.
- **Metric**: Number of downloads of comparison materials and engagement with case studies.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Developers decide to test Build-Accelerator to evaluate its performance.
- **Friction Points**: Installation challenges; initial setup complexity; lack of immediate results.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with apprehension; concern about time investment.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer a guided setup process; provide quick-start guides and video tutorials; ensure responsive customer support during trial.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and time taken to complete the setup.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Developers experience significant improvements in compilation speed and productivity during the trial period.
- **Friction Points**: Integration with existing tools may present challenges; potential resistance from team members.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction and relief; increased confidence in the tool; desire to share with peers.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Facilitate easy integration with popular development tools; provide onboarding sessions for teams; create community forums for sharing tips and best practices.
- **Metric**: Number of active users and retention rate post-trial.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Developers and teams recognize the value of Build-Accelerator and seek to leverage its capabilities further.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about advanced features; potential for feature overload.
- **User Emotions**: Enthusiasm for new possibilities; desire for continuous improvement.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer advanced training sessions; provide personalized recommendations based on usage patterns; introduce a referral program for teams to share the tool with others.
- **Metric**: Number of users upgrading to premium features and referrals generated.
